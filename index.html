About Me

I am an engineering student at Trinity College Dublin, passionate about designing and building. I love every aspect of engineering, blending problem-solving with creativity. From early programming projects in Scratch to participating in CTYI’s Early University Entrance program, I have continually pushed myself to expand my knowledge and skills. My current focus is on electronic and computer engineering, where I aim to specialise in my third year of college.

Clubs

Formula Trinity

As a member of the powertrain team for Formula Trinity, our college's team for the Formula Student competition in the UK, I have honed my skills in CAD design, fluid flow simulations, and hands-on manufacturing. My responsibilities include welding, angle grinding, plasma cutting, and implementing the parts I design. I successfully earned the 'Rookie of the Year' award for being the best newcomer, highlighting the significant value of my contributions to the team.

For more information about the team, check out the Formula Trinity Website
